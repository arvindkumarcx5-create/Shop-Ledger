Recommendations + Shopping advice

every piece you bring home should earn its place. here’s how to choose — and where.

gomel market · second saturday

① flax & memory · belarusian linen

shopping advice — linen

  • look for "lyon" or " льон" — local mills (Kupalinka, Baranavichy).
  • avoid blends with synthetics; pure linen breathes and lasts.
  • wash once before wearing — it softens, and shrinkage happens.
  • tip: buy undyed if possible; ecological dyes age beautifully.
📍 known stockists: Krynica (vul. Jakuba Kolasa 24), Veras flea every Sunday.

② winter wool · buy with intention

advice: before you buy a sweater

  • check fibre composition: at least 80% wool, ideally local sheep (rough feel = warm).
  • inspect seams — hand-linked collars last longer.
  • shop march/april: end‑of‑season discounts, but also less choice.
  • ask at Niamiha second‑floor — a few babushkas sell handknits.
✧ moth deterrent: cedar blocks or dried lavender — no naphthalene.

③ cotton dress · five‑pocket philosophy

advice: dress hunting

  • pockets are non‑negotiable (if you want them).
  • check armhole depth — raise your arms (in the dressing room).
  • second‑hand gems: “Haspadar” on Čyrvanona, 50‑70% less.
  • deadstock fabric dresses occasionally at “KaliLaska”.
💡 GOTS certification is great, but small local makers often use organic without the expensive label.

④ denim · raw, rigid, repairable

advice: find your denim

  • feel the weight: 12–14 oz is everyday durable.
  • skip pre‑distressed; you earn fades yourself.
  • vintage levi’s pop up at “Łёс” second‑hand (vul. Kisialiova 12).
  • repair café at “HUB Minsk” can fix ripped knees (small donation).
✧ wash denim only when truly dirty — spot clean, inside out, cold.

⑤ boots built to resole

advice: before you invest

  • turn the boot over — visible stitching (Goodyear welt) means recraftable.
  • leather should smell like leather, not chemicals.
  • try late morning — feet swell; bring your own thick socks.
  • cobbler on Niezaliežnasci 44 replaces heels for ≈25 руб.
👢 wax with beeswax blend, not silicone — leather stays breathable.

⑥ silk scarf · how to spot real

advice: burn test (if seller allows a thread)

  • real silk smells like burnt hair, synthetic melts into plastic bead.
  • vintage bins at “Clothes for Friends” (vul. Raduzhnaya 8) — often real silk mixed.
  • printed scarves from 80s are often pure silk; check for rolled hems.
🪡 tie one on your bag — instant outfit lift.

⑦ mending kit · what to buy

advice: start with these

  • darning mushroom or a small wooden spoon (to hold area).
  • embroidery needles (size 5–9) + contrasting cotton thread.
  • scrap fabric for patches — old shirts work perfectly.
  • buy at “Zolka” haberdashery, vul. Kirava 25 — very cheap.
✧ visible mending is storytelling — learn running stitch, catch stitch.

shopping manifesto (short)

🧺 cost per wear: divide price by expected wears. 200 руб boots / 500 days = 0.4 руб/day.
🗺️ local first: within 50km — less transport, easier to repair.
📏 try it twice: if you still think about it after 48h, return.
🔄 bring a list: “do i need another striped shirt?” (probably not).
